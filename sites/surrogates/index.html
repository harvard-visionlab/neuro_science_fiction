Model Guide - What's the difference between these models?

Different models may produce different ratings - this variation is expected and can be valuable for understanding feature reliability.

gemini-2.5-flash (Google) - Fast, efficient model optimized for quick responses. Designed for straightforward tasks.
gemini-2.5-pro (Google) - Advanced version with enhanced reasoning capabilities. May provide more detailed responses for complex features.
gpt-5 (OpenAI) - Large-scale language model with broad knowledge across domains. Generally considered OpenAI's most capable general-purpose model.
gpt-5-mini (OpenAI) - Compact version designed to balance efficiency with capability. Good starting point for most rating tasks.
o3-mini (OpenAI) - Specialized reasoning model that uses extended thinking processes. Designed for tasks requiring analytical judgment.
